LibertarianValues 3.0

What is LibertarianValues?

LibertarianValues is a quiz inspired by the LibertarianValues by polittest quiz, designed to identify your position on the libertarian spectrum. While originally aimed at libertarians, it is suitable for anyone in the LibRight or LibLeft parts of the political compassâ€”that is, anyone who favors minimal or decentralized authority rather than authoritarianism. You will be presented with statements and asked to indicate your opinion, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer affecting your scores. Questions are presented by axis in order, and at the end, your responses are compared to the maximum possible values to give a percentage. Answer honestly!

There are 102 questions in the test.

What are the values?

There are currently six axes, each of which has two opposing values. They are:

Authority vs. Anarchy

Hierarchy vs. Heterarchy

Planning vs. Markets

Collectivism vs. Individualism

Horizontal Unity vs. Vertical Unity

Nature vs. Technology

Conservatism vs. Progressivism

Interventionism vs. isolationism

Propertarianism vs. Georgism

What does "Closest Match" mean in the results?

Similar to 8values, this quiz will attempt to match you with a specific libertarian ideology. There are currently fourteen possible ideologies, with more to come in the future. This is a work in progress and may not work as intended. Suggestions are very welcome.
